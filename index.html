Knowledge Of Branches Of Government Decreased

After years of steady increase, the percentage of people who can name all three branches of government decreased 16% from 2021 to 2022, while the percentage of people who couldn't name any branches of government increased 25% during the same time period.

Source: Annenberg Constitution Day Civics Survey


Knowledge Of First Amendment Rights Declined

The percentage of people who could name individual First Amendment rights decreased from 2021 to 2022, while the percentage of people who couldn't name any rights increased 53% during the same time period.
